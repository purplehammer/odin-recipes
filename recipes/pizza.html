Pizza

Description

Mushroom and black olive pizza is a savory dish featuring a crisp crust, a layer of rich tomato sauce, and a blanket of melted mozzarella cheese. Sliced mushrooms provide a subtle, earthy taste, while black olives contribute a sharp, salty contrast. This pizza is often garnished with Italian herbs before being baked to perfection, resulting in a classic flavor combination beloved by many.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza crust
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup sliced black olives
  • 1 teaspoon Italian herbs

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
  2. Spread tomato sauce over pizza crust.
  3. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over sauce.
  4. Top with mushrooms and olives.
  5. Sprinkle with Italian herbs.
  6. Bake in preheated oven until cheese and crust are golden brown, about 15 minutes.

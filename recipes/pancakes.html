Pancakes

Description

Blueberry pancakes are fluffy breakfast treats studded with juicy blueberries. The batter, a blend of flour, eggs, milk, and leavening agents, is lightly mixed with the blueberries and spooned onto a hot griddle. Cooked until golden brown and served in stacks, they're typically topped with syrup and occasionally extra butter, offering a burst of blueberry flavor with each sweet and tender bite.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, sift together flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. In a small bowl, beat together egg and milk. Stir milk and egg into flour mixture. Mix in the butter and fold in the blueberries. Set aside for 1 hour.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot.
